## Financial Metrics

### Gross Margins
- Industry Average: 70-85%
- Top Performers: >80%
- Services Heavy: 60-70%

### Operating Expenses
(As % of revenue)
- R&D: 15-25%
- Sales & Marketing: 40-60%
- G&A: 15-20%

### Cash Flow
- Burn Multiple: 1.0-1.5
- CAC Payback: 12-18 months
- Rule of 40: >40%

## Efficiency Metrics

### Sales Efficiency
- Magic Number: >1.0
- Sales Cycle: 30-90 days
- Win Rate: 20-30%

### Customer Success
- Churn Rate: <2% monthly
- NPS Score: >40
- CSAT Score: >90%
